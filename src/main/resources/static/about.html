Home About us

Finding what you exactly need in short time at one place is very hard. Say you are bored and you want to look for some top rated movies online or you want to go watch something in theatres. What would you do? Probably go to two different websites for each? It’s still ok to go search in two websites. But let’s say you want to order some food as well. Would you prefer looking for a third site? Have you ever thought how much of your time would it save if all these were on a single website. This is what we are planning to build.


Who doesn’t want everything to be available in one place? It’s a busy world, not everyone has time to go through ten sites and look for different products, so I think our web app comes in very handy here and saves a lot of time. Just login once and you get everything. It just makes life much easier and you can use this time to play, have some fun.

Vision & Misssion

Our goal is to make everything accessible under one website. Our web app is going to be a platform to people who can host everything that falls under one domain. For instance, we will build an app that contains details of all the movies. People can view the top rated movies under some particular genre. Then we’ll also add features that will allow users to view movies in the nearby theatres and book tickets for the same
